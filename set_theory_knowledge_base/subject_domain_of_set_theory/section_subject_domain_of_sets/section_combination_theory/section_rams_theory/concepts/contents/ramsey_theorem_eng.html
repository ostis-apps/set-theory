Formulation in the language of graph theory

For any c natural numbers n1, n2, … , nc any c-colored edge coloring of a sufficiently large complete graph contains a complete subgraph with ni vertices for some color i. In particular, for any r and s, a sufficiently large complete graph of two-color (black-and-white) coloring contains either a complete black subgraph of r vertices , or a complete white subgraph of s vertices.
