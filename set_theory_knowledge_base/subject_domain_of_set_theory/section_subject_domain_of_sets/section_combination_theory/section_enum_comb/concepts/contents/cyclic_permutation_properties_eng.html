According to one of the fundamental properties of symmetric groups, any permutation can be represented as a product of disjoint cycles. These cycles can be rearranged with respect to each other, and the expression of the permutation is unique up to the order of the cycles. The multiset of cycle lengths (the cycle type) is uniquely determined by the permutation.
